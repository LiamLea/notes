### Overview

#### 1.Concepts

##### (1) Metadata (The Foundation)
It defines the structure, schema, and descriptive information about data assets.

##### (2) usage
Usage metadata tells you how the data is being consumed and by whom

##### (3) lineage
Lineage tracks the movement of data over time

##### (4) profiler
The profiler provides insights into the quality and characteristics of the data, such as data types, distributions, and anomalies.
